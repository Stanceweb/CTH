Frequently asked questions

Practical answers, not placeholder copy.

How long does migration take?

Most standard website migrations are completed within 24 to 72 hours, depending on the size and complexity of the site.

What is your backup retention policy?

Backups are performed automatically and retained based on the active hosting plan.

What is your refund policy?

Refund eligibility depends on the service term, payment timing, and account standing. A plain-language summary is available on the refund policy page.

How does billing work?

You can choose monthly or annual billing. Intro pricing may apply to the first term, and renewals follow the applicable standard rate. Customers manage subscriptions and invoices in the account billing area.

Is SSL included?

Yes. Free SSL is included and provisioned as part of the managed hosting setup.

How fast is support response?

We aim to respond as quickly as possible, with priority based on issue severity and service impact.
